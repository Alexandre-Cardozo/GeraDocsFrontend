Action button in GeraDocs' five variants; use `primary` for the main action on a view, `secondary` for neutral actions, `success` for final/generate steps.

```jsx
<Button variant="primary" icon={<PlusIcon />}>Novo Processo</Button>
<Button variant="secondary">Voltar</Button>
<Button variant="success">Finalizar e Gerar Documento</Button>
```

Variants: primary | secondary | dark | success | ghost. Sizes: sm | md (36px) | lg. Labels often end with "→".
